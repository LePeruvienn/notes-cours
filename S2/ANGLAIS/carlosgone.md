## 1. What where the pros and cons of his management and leadership ?

**Turnaround expertise (hard results, not theory)**

Ghosn became famous for rescuing Nissan in the late 1990s. At the time, Nissan was near collapse. His Nissan Revival Plan:

- Cut costs aggressively
- Closed factories
- Reduced suppliers
- Paid down massive debt

**Decisive, data-driven leadership**

- Clear targets
- Accountability culture
- Performance metrics

This contrasted sharply with traditional Japanese consensus-based management.
- But it also clashed wit the locals norm

**Pros and Cons**

Ghosn was :

- Highly effective in crisis turnaround
- Strongly analytical and decisive
- Comfortable operating across cultures

But also:

- Over-reliant on personal authority
- Potentially blind to governance risks
- Only partially culturally adaptive
